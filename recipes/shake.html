The Great Chocolate Mint Milk Shake

One of the all time greats, the Mint Chocolate Milk Shake. Cool and refreshing.


Prep Time: 5 Mins
Total Time: 5 Mins
Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops vanilla or chocolate ice cream
  • 1 cup white sugar/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chocolate syrup (to taste)
  • 1 drop peppermint extract

Steps

  1. Combine ice cream, milk, chocolate syrup, and peppermint extract in a blender; blend until smooth.

  2. Pour into 2 glasses and serve.
